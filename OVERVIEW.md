# Jeremy OS — Overview

> **Reduce noise. Increase clarity. Move the mountain.**

A mobile-first Progressive Web App that works like a personal operating system
for focus, identity, and recovery — not a habit tracker, not a productivity app.
It's built to feel like a calm, premium, dark-mode mentor: large type,
voice-first, fast, and private. No guilt, no shame, no red warnings — only
awareness.

---

## The daily experience

- **Mission Control** — *"Who am I becoming?"* Your editable identity statement
  greets you every day, anchoring the work to who you're choosing to be.
- **Today's Mountain** — exactly **one** goal. No lists, no competing
  priorities. Everything else is noise.
- **Pressure Meter** — a calm 1–10 read on what you're carrying, plus what's
  driving it (financial, client, team, future, and more), trended over time.
- **Morning Check-In** — voice-first. Speak what's true, name the mountain,
  name the pressure, define the win — and get an AI-reflected summary to start
  clear.
- **Evening Reflection** — close the day honestly: the win, the heaviest
  pressure, whether you moved the mountain, and what you learned.

## Awareness, not judgment

- **Key habits we're forming and nurturing** — quiet, private check-ins that
  build self-awareness around the patterns you're working to change. Logged
  discreetly, stored privately, never with shame or streak-pressure. The goal
  is understanding, not punishment.
- **The AI Recovery Coach** — a coach, mirror, and strategist (never a
  therapist, never diagnosing). It reads your recent weeks and helps you connect
  the dots between pressure, sleep, energy, focus, and the habits you're
  nurturing — then points at the single next move.

## Seeing the bigger picture

- **Patterns** — visual charts and auto-detected correlations across pressure,
  sleep, weight, focus, and your daily habits, over weekly / monthly / quarterly
  ranges. Patterns are signals, not verdicts.
- **Manumation Command Center** — the summit. Funnel, content, outbound,
  planning, and team readiness all roll up into one number that matters:
  **Distance to Launch.**

## Under the hood

- **Next.js + TypeScript + Tailwind**, installable as a real app (PWA) that
  works offline.
- **Single-user by design** — it's yours. Data lives instantly on-device and
  syncs to a private **Postgres** database, deployed on **Railway**.
- **Optional AI** via OpenAI (currently `gpt-5.4-mini`), with a built-in offline
  coaching engine so it always works.

## The point

The purpose isn't to white-knuckle anything away overnight. It's to **reduce
noise, build awareness, create singular focus, and launch Manumation** —
becoming the person capable of sustaining success once it arrives.
